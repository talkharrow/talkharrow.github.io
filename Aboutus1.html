About "Talk: Harrow"

A number of independent local groups of people
are meeting in the Harrow area to encourage
those for whom English is not their first
language.

Learners may be new to English or already fluent
Leaders may be ESOL qualified and
may offer teaching in written English too. Others
are trained by SpEC spec-London.org.uk
to facilitate Conversation Clubs, and some are
'just' volunteers, learning 'on the job'.

"Talk: Harrow" is a proposed new network to
enable us to support each other. It is not just to
about imposing any structure or 'system',
support people in setting up and running groups
in the ways that suit them best. Hopefully we can
share resources, useful web links, etc., and help
learner to find the group, or groups they need
other as appropriate.

Your suggestions and feedback are very welcome
but please note this is currently an entirely
voluntary initiative; We will respond as soon as
we can .
