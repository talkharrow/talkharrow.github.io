There is scope
to set up new
groups or run
current groups
more
frequently...
...but we need
your help!
PLEASE get in touch if you have time you can
contribute. We will negotiate a role for you,
whoever you are! Currently seeking the following,
but do offer any other ideas you think
would be of use to the network.
- Group conveners- The people who make it happen,
by organising the venue, liasing with host
organisation, managing publicity and resources
and welcoming learners.
- Group facilitators- Welcome the learners,
introduce and oversee the activities,
monitoring, record keeping, etc
- Activity leaders- Those who give some thought to
preparations (a topic for the session, a game
or other activity) and bring any necessary equipment
so they can deliver that activity at the session
- Publicity production - Posters,website,
social media, videos etc.
- Translators for all the above, in all languages
URGENTLY NEEDED!
- Communications, 'in house'- emailing or texting
learners with dates, reminders and requests to help
the group as appropriate
- Communications, external- Liasing with schools,
places of worship, community groups,
media, other agencies as appropriate
- Researchers- Gathering other
resources to share here
